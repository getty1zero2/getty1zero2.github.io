About Me

I am a Colorado School of Mines-educated Mechanical Engineer and certified EIT with over 8 years of experience specializing in autonomous vehicle assemblies and high-pressure process machines. My expertise lies in designing IP-rated enclosures and thermal management systems using advanced CAD and CFD tools, while leading cross-functional reviews to ensure cost-effective fabrication and streamlined integration. I am eager to leverage my background in DFM/DFA and rapid prototyping to create high-performance, cost-effective mechanical solutions for your design team.
